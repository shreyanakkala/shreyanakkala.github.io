Helpful/Insightful Data Visualization

Source: https://archive.nytimes.com/www.nytimes.com/interactive/2012/02/13/us/politics/2013-budget-proposal-graphic.html?

This interactive visualization provides all sorts of information about the budget spending which I think is insightful. It does not show any bias and that makes the viewer come up with their own opinions. It shows the numbers and allows the viewer to find the specific information they are looking for. It also presents multiple different views of the graph to display the information differently.

Bad/Unhelpful Data Visualization

Source: https://www.politifact.com/factchecks/2015/oct/01/jason-chaffetz/chart-shown-planned-parenthood-hearing-misleading-/

This graph is unhelpful because it does not have a Y axis sigifying the exact amount of increase or decrease. When looking at it the audience just sees the steep lines which are not accurate. The cancer screenings are plotted in the millions whereas the abortions are plotted in the thousands which distorts the numbers due to the way the graph is plotted. There is no relationship there but it forces the viewer to see a relationship between cancer screenings and abortions performed.
